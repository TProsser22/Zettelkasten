## 7100.25.5 — Experience, The Effect of Time on

The passage of time really impacts the value and effectiveness of our past experience. Most obviously, as time passes things change. What worked in the past may not be relevant due to shifts in competition, innovation, or emergent trends or complexities. ^173^ But also, we are not patient with the lessons of our experience. We can make faulty choices or assumptions when we don't leave time for things to "play out." Like randomness, the time delay between a cause & its effect may lead to false stories and be unhelpful guides in future decisions. ^40^
