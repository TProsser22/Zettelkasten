## 5319.4.25 — Emergence in Complexity

Systems exhibiting complex behaviours & patterns that arise unpredictably from its simpler components are experiencing emergence. The simpler components are interconnected and evolving in ways that appear highly organized without any definable "guiding hand". No mathematical [model] can predict emergence. For example, the US Stock Market routinely exhibits emergence.
