## 2323A.50.50 — Sequence of Return Risk

An average return rate on investments (or even gambling) isn't very relevant if you experience randomness and volatility early in a time sequence — you may just go broke! Some financial models look Ergodic (`4204.50.40`) but are not. With retirement savings, this could result in running out of money before retirement ends. The concept of expected returns is really meaningless for an individual.
